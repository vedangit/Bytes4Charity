Thousands of young girls don't have access to sanitary products

Let's fix that together.


A Period of Pride





For many of these girls, managing their menstrual hygiene is a daily struggle due to financial constraints.

Our project seeks to provide these girls with the sanitary products they need to maintain their dignity, health, and confidence. By offering free sanitary products and education on menstrual hygiene, we hope to empower these girls to continue their education and daily activities without interruption.
